Who We Are

Africa Tomorrow Project (ATP) (Nonprofit)

Motto: Power for the Future

Long Term Objective:

It is the goal of Africa Tomorrow Project (ATP) to be an integral partner in providing good health to Ghanaians living in rural areas. We plan to achieve this in the next fifteen to twenty years through partnership with stakeholders and interested entities in provision of quality affordable healthcare in rural communities in Ghana.

Objectives: Short term (Annually):

  • To partner with healthcare providers and educators to create awareness on issues pertaining to the health of the citizens of Ghana.
  • To partner with various organizations and authorities to educate the youth on challenging issues they face in their day to day activities.
  • Goals will be achieved through our executive members. ATP will not hand money to an individual or an organization.

Mission: Create awareness on societal issues, Partner with educators and health groups in our communities to improve lives, To partner with organizations who have the interest of the youth at heart.
